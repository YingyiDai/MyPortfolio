a game inspired by the movie, Pacific Rim.

The movie, Pacific Rim

At that time, Pacific Rim was hot and I went to see it with my friends.

We were all interseted in the way people control the big robots in the movie. They couple as a team, each one control half part and must be in great consistence.

So we decided to build a game playing with two people controlling only one robot like this.

Game Concept

We used two smartphone as the controller for each side of the robot. Though its technology we can transfer the movement into control data. If both smartphone move for the similar way, the robot will move faster. In othervice the robot will move very slowly.
